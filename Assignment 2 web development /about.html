About BELLY UP

Your Melbourne food discovery companion

Who We Are

BELLY UP is a Melbourne-based restaurant discovery website made for students, locals and food lovers who want to find great places to eat without spending hours searching online.

What We Do

Our website helps users explore restaurants, cafes and dessert spots across Melbourne. You can browse recommendations, discover new food places and reserve a table easily.

Why BELLY UP?

Whether you are looking for a quick lunch, a cosy dinner spot or a weekend food adventure, BELLY UP makes it simple to choose the right restaurant based on your mood and preferences.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a simple, user-friendly and enjoyable food discovery experience for people living, studying or visiting Melbourne.
